BADMENTON

Badminton is a racquet sport played either individually (singles) or in teams of two (doubles). Players use a shuttlecock and racquet to hit the shuttlecock over a net, aiming to land it within the opponent's court. The game can be played indoors or outdoors.

HOW TO PLAY BADMINTON:

1. Setup: Players stand on opposite sides of a net.
2. Scoring: Points can be scored on every serve (rally scoring) and are awarded when the shuttlecock lands in the opponent's court.
3. Serve: The game starts with a serve, which must be hit underhand and land in the correct service box.
4. Gameplay: Players rally the shuttlecock back and forth, aiming to score points by landing it in the opponent's court.
