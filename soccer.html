SOCCER

also known as football in most countries, is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players each on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. The objective is to score by getting the ball into the opposing teamâ€™s goal using any part of the body except the hands and arms (except for the goalkeeper).

HOW TO PLAY SOCCER:

1. Objective: Score more goals than the opposing team within the match duration (90 minutes, divided into two 45-minute halves).
2. Gameplay: Players pass, dribble, and shoot the ball while defending their goal. Only goalkeepers can use their hands within their penalty area.
3. Rules: Common rules include no handball, offsides, fouls, and throw-ins when the ball leaves the field's sidelines.
4. Restarts: Play restarts with throw-ins, goal kicks, or corner kicks depending on how and where the ball leaves the field.
