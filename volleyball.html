Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport where two teams of six aim to score by hitting a ball over a net into the opponent's court. Teams have up to three touches to return the ball, and matches are played in sets, typically to 25 points. It emphasizes teamwork, skill, and strategy.

How to Play Volleyball

Teams are divided into two sides, with a net in the middle. Points are scored when the opposing team fails to return the ball or when the ball lands in their court. Players rotate positions after winning the serve from the opposing team. Teams have three hits to return the ball over the net, typically using a bump, set, and spike.

Fundamentals of Volleyball

  • Passing: Using forearms to pass the ball to a teammate.
  • Setting: Using fingers to set the ball up for a spike.
  • Spiking: Hitting the ball forcefully over the net.
  • Blocking: Preventing the ball from crossing the net.
